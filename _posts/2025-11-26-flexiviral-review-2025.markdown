## What is FlexiViral?

FlexiViral is a cloud-based lead-generation app that automates a referral reward loop. Rather than passively capturing emails, FlexiViral gives each new subscriber a unique referral link and rewards for sharing. Rewards can be digital files, videos, badges, or unlocks — whatever motivates your audience to share. The system tracks referrals, shows real-time leaderboards, and automates email follow-ups and reward delivery.

Think Dropbox’s “refer-a-friend” loop applied to email list building, giveaways and lead magnets — with leaderboards, fraud protection and agency features built in.

---

## How FlexiViral’s viral loop works (simple 4-step system)

1. **Signup** — A visitor opts in to your campaign (email + referral link generated).
2. **Share** — The new lead shares their unique link to earn rewards.
3. **Refer** — Friends click, sign up and unlock rewards for referrer + friend.
4. **Repeat** — Each new subscriber becomes another referrer — growth compounds.
